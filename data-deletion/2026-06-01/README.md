**Data Deletion Request**

This page explains how to request the deletion of your personal data for the Spensa app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by Francisco Javier Ponce Rosales (collectively, the "Application"). Francisco Javier Ponce Rosales is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider".

This page complements the Application's Privacy Policy, which describes how your personal data is collected, used, and retained.

**What this request does**

When you submit a data deletion request, the Service Provider will delete the personal data associated with you and your use of the Application, including User Provided Data such as your email address, name, profile picture, Google Drive data, and location, subject to any legal obligation to retain certain data. Please note that some data may be required by law to be retained for a limited period, and aggregated or anonymized data that no longer identifies you may be retained.

**How to request deletion of your data**

To request the deletion of your personal data, send an email to the Service Provider following these steps:

1.  Open your email client and create a new message.
2.  Send the email to: **mortalswat@gmail.com**
3.  In the **Subject** field, write: **DELETE DATA**
4.  In the body of the message, include the following information so the Service Provider can identify your account and process the request:
    *   The email address associated with your Spensa account
    *   Your name as registered in the Application (if applicable)
    *   A brief statement confirming that you are requesting the deletion of your personal data
5.  Send the email.

**Example email**

*   **To:** mortalswat@gmail.com
*   **Subject:** DELETE DATA
*   **Body:** I request the deletion of all personal data associated with my Spensa account. My account email address is: [your email address].

**What happens after you submit your request**

*   The Service Provider will acknowledge your request and may contact you to verify your identity before processing it, in order to protect your data from unauthorized deletion requests.
*   Once your identity is confirmed, the Service Provider will delete your personal data within a reasonable period, except for data that must be retained to comply with applicable law.
*   You will be notified once the deletion has been completed.

**Before you submit your request**

*   Deleting your data is permanent and cannot be undone. After deletion, you may lose access to your account and any content associated with it.
*   Uninstalling the Application stops further collection of data from your device, but it does not automatically delete data that has already been transmitted to the Service Provider. To delete that data, you must submit a request as described above.
*   For more information about your rights and how your data is handled, please review the Application's Privacy Policy.

**Contact Us**

If you have any questions about requesting the deletion of your data, please contact the Service Provider via email at mortalswat@gmail.com.
